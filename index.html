TrafficCamNZ

Access live traffic camera feeds and web images with effortless precision. Curated from over 5,000 cameras worldwide, TrafficCamNZ lets you build and manage personalized lists—featured views in 1, 4, or 16-image grids, with seamless scrolling and auto-refresh.

Effortless Camera Management

Build your list with bulk import/export via XML. Feature your favorites for instant grid display. Drag to reorder, add any web image—traffic cams, weather maps, or custom sources—all under your control.

Native macOS Interactions

Single-click fullscreen, double-click refresh, right-click menus. Intuitive drag & drop for exports and reordering. Smart tooltips guide every action, replacing shortcuts with natural Mac fluency.

Revolutionary Auto-Refresh

Visual countdown ring tracks the next update. Segmented controls for 15s to 5m intervals—click to cycle, long-press for custom. Intelligent scheduling ensures fresh images without network strain.

Pixel-Perfect Performance

Dynamic layouts adapt to your camera count. Swift-rewritten for reliability, with CloudKit sync across Mac and iOS. Intelligent caching, background processing, and modern layer backing deliver smooth, current visuals.

Professional Polish

Modern animations, redesigned splash, and native macOS design. Faster startups, zero crashes, optimized memory—built for daily reliability on macOS Sequoia and beyond.

How It Works

1. Build Your List

Import cameras via XML or add web images manually. Select featured ones and drag to reorder for your perfect grid.

2. View in Grid

Display 1, 4, or 16 images. Scroll left/right, single-click fullscreen, double-click to refresh instantly.

3. Auto-Refresh Mastery

Set intervals with segmented controls. Watch the progress ring countdown to the next update—always current.

4. Sync and Export

CloudKit keeps lists synced with iOS. Drag & drop images to export, right-click for quick actions.
